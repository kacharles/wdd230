Website Planning Document

Site Name

The site will be called Lefkosa Chamber of Commerce. The site will be hosted on the following domain: lefkosachamber.com.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to bring together local businesses in Lefkosa to network, support, and promote business activities. The site will serve has a hub connecting local businesses to promote and advertise products and services in the area. The site will aslo be used to display individual buisiness services and products or contain the website of each business that is part of the chamber.

Target Audience

The targeted audience for this site are local businesses owner in Lefkosa who are seeking to grow their business and establish an online presence through networking and colaboration.

Scenarios
  • How does the Chamber support individual businesses?
  • What type of businesses do the Chamber support?
  • What are the prerequiste for joing the Chamber?
